More About Me

In 2013 I graduated with a degree in Professional Writing from the University of Oklahoma. I didn't find work in my field, instead landing a job in Colorado managing the US dealer network for a company that manufactures audiophile stereo gear.


Despite having no sales experience, I learned to be an effective salesperson quickly, nearly doubling the profit from my dealers during my 5 year tenure. The problem was, I knew it wasn't for me.


I am, at my core, a problem solver and sales did not scratch that itch. I'm happiest when I’m in the weeds on a problem, working through it systematically to find a solution. That’s why I like coding as much as I do.


When I’m away from my computer, you’ll likely find me cycling, reading, gaming, playing chess, or doing yoga.
